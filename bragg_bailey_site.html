How we operate
Bragg & Bailey runs on a simple, repeatable process that keeps expectations clear for both seniors and their families.

1. Discovery call

A brief phone conversation to understand the situation, devices involved, and any urgency. Family members or caregivers are welcome on the call.

2. Written agreement

Before the visit, we provide a concise, plain-language summary: what we will work on, what it will cost, and any limitations.

3. On-site execution & documentation

During the visit we document key changes, capture photos where appropriate, and leave behind simple reference notes if useful.

4. Follow-up

We reach out after the engagement to confirm everything is working and answer any short questions related to the visit.

5. Governance & privacy

We treat personal information, passwords, and accounts with the same care we would for our own family. Sensitive details are never stored unnecessarily.

6. Relationship-driven

Many clients work with us on a recurring basis as technology changes. Our documentation makes each subsequent visit faster and more predictable.
